ABOUT US

Welcome to BOW, the premier women's pre-professional organization at Duke University!

WHO

We are a group of business oriented females in the Duke University community promoting education, experience, and exchange between interested students and outstanding industry professionals.

WHAT

Our objective is to motivate and prepare young women for careers in all types of business, be it advertising, consulting, finance, marketing, or public relations.

HOW

We cultivate student-led forums, skill-building workshops, and various guest speaker events that exemplify the triumphs of professional women and guide their personal and professional development.
